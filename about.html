About this site

Built for couples who want real answers, not generic checklists.

Marriages In My City is a local wedding planning resource. We write specific, researched guides for real cities — the venues that actually exist there, the license process at the actual county clerk, the seasonal realities, the things nobody puts in a checklist.

📍

Local, not generic

Every city guide is written for that city. Miami wedding planning is not the same as Albany wedding planning. We treat each location as its own editorial subject — climate, culture, license process, venue style, guest logistics, and all.

✍️

Editorial standards

Our content is researched, reviewed, and updated. For anything that changes — marriage license requirements, clerk office hours, filing fees — we always direct you to confirm with the official source. We point, we don't certify.

🤖

Honest about AI

Wendy, our wedding concierge, is powered by AI. We're transparent about that. She's trained to give practical, local-aware guidance — not to pretend to be human, not to book anything on your behalf, and not to replace your own judgment.

Your data stays yours

Your Bridal Blueprint lives in your browser by default. We don't sell your data, we don't share it with advertisers, and we tell you exactly what we do and don't collect. Read our Privacy Policy for the specifics.

Meet Wendy

Wendy is the AI wedding concierge built into every page of Marriages In My City. She's designed to help couples think through local planning decisions: which venue style fits the city, when to start booking vendors, what questions to ask the clerk's office, how to structure a realistic timeline.

She works best when you tell her where you're getting married, roughly when, and what kind of wedding you're imagining. From there she helps you build a Bridal Blueprint — a personalized local plan that's yours to refer back to.

Wendy does not book anything, confirm anything, or replace a licensed planner or attorney. She's a thinking partner, not a booking agent. We explain the full scope of how AI is used on this site in our AI Disclosure.

Our editorial approach

Each city and state guide on Marriages In My City covers the same core territory: marriage license path, venue styles, seasonal realities, guest logistics, vendor booking sequence, and local planning context. But the content is specific to the city, not templated from a generic base.

We don't publish venue listings, reviews, or vendor recommendations that couples should treat as endorsements. The venues and vendors mentioned are illustrative of a category or style — not referrals. Always do your own vetting.

Content is updated on a rolling basis. The date shown on each page reflects the most recent review. If you find something that's out of date or inaccurate, please email us — we take that seriously.
