How to use this directory

Start broad, then narrow quickly. Pick the venue style that best matches the feeling of the day, then pressure-test the logistics: weather backup, guest movement, vendor access, transportation, noise limits, budget, and what is actually included in the venue fee.

Once a couple knows the type of setting they want, Wendy can help compare local fit by city, date, guest count, budget, ceremony needs, family priorities, and vendor requirements.
