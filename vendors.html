How to use this vendor directory

Start broad, then narrow quickly. Pick the categories that control the day first: venue, food and beverage, planner or coordinator, photography, music, beauty, florals, transportation, and any specialty vendor that matters to your family or ceremony. Open the category guide, read the comparison questions, search your city, and save promising local options into your Bridal Blueprint.

Until Marriage In My City has independent listings for every vendor category in every city, the Google-powered tile search gives couples a practical bridge. It lets them discover local businesses, open profiles or websites, and keep the planning notes in one place instead of losing everything across tabs.
