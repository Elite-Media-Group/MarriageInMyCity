Privacy Policy

Marriages In My City is designed to help couples organize wedding planning details. This V1 may store planning details on your device through local browser storage. If account sync, email reminders, or partner integrations are connected later, those features should be governed by an updated policy and consent flow.

Planning information

Wedding city, dates, guest counts, notes, vendor ideas, registry links, and similar details may be entered by users. Do not enter sensitive information you do not want stored on your device.

Email and reminders

Email reminders should only be sent when a user provides clear consent.
