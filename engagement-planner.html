What is the Engagement Planner?

The Engagement Planner is the post-yes hub that turns a happy blur into a clear, calm plan. It keeps your timeline, budget, guests, vendors and milestones in one place so wedding planning feels guided rather than overwhelming.

How to use this page: Skim the planning lanes below, then open the planner with Wendy to turn each lane into real tasks built around your city, date and guest count.

Planning timeline

Most couples plan over nine to fifteen months, but the right timeline is the one that fits your date and your life. Use these phases as a calm order of operations.

Foundations

Set a rough budget, a guest-count range and your non-negotiables before booking anything.

Big bookings

Lock the venue and date, then the vendors that book out earliest in your city.

Details

Attire, design, menu tastings, invitations and the guest experience come together here.

Final weeks

Confirm counts, finalize the timeline with vendors, and hand off the day-of details.

Budget planner overview

A budget is a tool that protects what matters most. A common starting split looks like this, then you shift it toward whatever you care about more.

Venue & catering 45% Photography & video 20% Flowers & decor 15% Attire & beauty 12% Everything else 8%

Read the full Wedding Budget Guide

Guest planning

Your guest count drives almost every other number. Decide the range first, agree on how invitations are split between families, and keep a single source of truth for RSVPs.

  • Set a realistic guest range before touring venues.
  • Agree early on who controls which portion of the list.
  • Track RSVPs, meals and accessibility needs in one place.

Vendor planning

Book the vendors that sell out first, then build outward. Local availability matters more than national averages, which is why your city guide is part of the plan.

Photographers Planners Florists DJs & Music Beauty Transport

Engagement Planner questions

Quick answers for couples who just said yes.

What is the Engagement Planner?

The Engagement Planner is the post-yes hub that turns your engagement into a clear plan: timeline, budget, guest list, vendors, a countdown and milestones, all in one place and guided by Wendy.

When should we start using it?

Right after the yes. Even before a date is set, the planner helps you frame priorities, a rough budget and the first milestones so nothing important slips.

Is the Engagement Planner free?

Yes. The Engagement Planner and Wendy's guidance are free to use. You bring your city, date and guest count, and the planner organizes the rest.

How does it connect to Before I Do?

Before I Do covers the pre-engagement, proposal stage. Once you are engaged, the Engagement Planner carries that moment into a full wedding plan.
