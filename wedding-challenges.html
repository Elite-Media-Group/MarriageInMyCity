Why this guide lane belongs on Marriages In My City

Wedding planning is not only vendors, venues, timelines, and flowers. It is people. And people are where the stress usually hides.

These guides are built for the moment a couple searches a question they do not want to ask out loud: how to handle an overbearing mother-in-law, what to do when friends are planning a bachelor party disaster, how to tell someone they are not invited, or how to keep social media from taking over the ceremony. Wendy’s role is to make the advice calm, specific, and usable.

Each page gives couples a quick answer, a plain-English plan, a script they can actually say, and a next step into their Bridal Blueprint.

Your city. Your guide. Your love story. Wendy helps couples plan with confidence, clarity, and local context.
